About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering with Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka at University of California, Berkeley. I am focused on building trustworthy planning algorithms for autonomous agents, such as vehicles and robots. Previously, I got my BS at Harbin Institute of Tehcnology, working with Prof. Huijun Gao and Prof. Weichao Sun on systems control and fault diagnosis.
